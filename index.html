What Super is for

Super focuses on personal AI agents that operate full computers — clicking, typing, navigating — and reuse a computer-use cache so repeated tasks get cheaper and faster over time.

Repeated workflows

For one‑off interactions, conversational tools can be enough. For recurring work like ops checks, lead research, or QA, Super’s cache‑backed approach compounds efficiency.
